How to play Close ×

  1. Click start quiz to begin the quiz
  2. Enter your name before you begin to play
  3. There will be one question, and four possible questions only one will be correct for every question.
  4. This is the ask the audience life line, it can only be used once, and be wary it may not always give the correct answer, especially when you're past question 5.
  5. This is the 50/50 life line, it will randomly take away to incorrect answers, again this can only be used once.
  6. This is the phone a friend life line, this life life can only be used once, and will also not give the correct answers the further along the quiz you go.
  7. This is the bank option, if you select this option the quiz will end, only use this option if you can't answer the question and have used all your life lines.
